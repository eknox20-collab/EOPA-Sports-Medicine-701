Depth of Knowledge

Understanding DOK Levels

The EOPA tests three levels of thinking. Knowing the difference helps you study smarter.

1
DOK Level 1
Recall & Reproduce
Remember facts, definitions, and basic concepts.
"What does RICE stand for?" · "Define hyponatremia."
2
DOK Level 2
Skills & Concepts
Apply knowledge, compare ideas, explain how or why.
"Why does cryotherapy reduce swelling?" · "How does Wolff's Law apply?"
3
DOK Level 3
Strategic Thinking
Analyze scenarios, evaluate options, draw conclusions.
"Which finding BEST indicates a Grade III ACL tear?"
